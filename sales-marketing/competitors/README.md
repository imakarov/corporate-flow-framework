## Analysis Template

When analyzing a competitor, include:

1. **Company Overview**
   - Product/service description
   - Target market
   - Company size and funding

2. **Positioning & Messaging**
   - Value proposition
   - Key messages
   - Differentiation claims

3. **Pricing**
   - Pricing model
   - Price points
   - Discounts/packages

4. **Sales Channels**
   - How they sell (direct, partners, self-serve)
   - Sales cycle length
   - Typical deal size

5. **Marketing Tactics**
   - Content strategy
   - Channels used
   - Campaign examples

6. **Strengths & Weaknesses**
   - What they do well
   - Where they're vulnerable
   - Our advantages vs them

7. **Battlecard**
   - Quick comparison points
   - Objection handling
   - Win strategies
