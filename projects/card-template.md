### 2. Quick Stats - Add Revenue Fields
```markdown
| **Monthly Revenue (MRR)** | $X,XXX/month |
| **Annual Revenue (ARR)** | $XXX,XXX/year |
| **% of Total Revenue** | X% |
| **Contract Start** | YYYY-MM-DD |
| **Contract Status** | Active / Renewal Due / At Risk |
```

### 3. Required Sections (Mandatory for Projects)

#### Active Projects
List all product deployments:
- Product/deployment name
- User counts
- Health status
- Location/region

#### Metrics & Feedback
Track usage and client health:
- Current usage metrics
- Client feedback (pain points + positive)
- Client health score (X/10)
- Risk level
- Action items

### 4. Remove Trial Information
Delete "Trial Information" section - not needed for paying clients.

### 5. Activity Log Focus
Focus on:
- Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)
- Feature requests and product feedback
- Support issues and resolutions
- Expansion/upsell opportunities
- Contract renewals
